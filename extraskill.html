Extra-Curricular and Skills

  • Critical Thinking: Developed analytical skills to address challenges related to information retrieval and data organization.
  • Project Management: Hands-on experience in managing team projects and events successfully.

These extra-curricular activities and skills have prepared me for real-world challenges, ensuring I can contribute effectively to library operations and the broader information management field.
