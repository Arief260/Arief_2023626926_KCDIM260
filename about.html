About Me

I am a Bachelor of Library Management student at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kedah. In this program, I am developing key skills and knowledge essential for managing library resources, organizing information, and ensuring effective access to information in various formats. The curriculum includes courses on information science, cataloging, database management, information retrieval, and digital libraries.

Being part of UiTM Kedah's Library Management program not only prepares me for traditional roles in library settings but also equips me to work in digital and information management sectors. I am learning to navigate the latest technologies and tools used in the field, preparing me for a career in today’s information-driven world.
